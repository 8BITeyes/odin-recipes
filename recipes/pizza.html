Pizza

Classic Homemade Pizza Recipe

You don't have to let your pizza dough rise overnight, but if you do, it will develop more flavor and air bubbles.

Make sure to take the dough out of the refrigerator an hour before using it to bring it to room temperature.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Pepperoni
  • Flour
  • Yeast

Steps

  1. Proof the yeast
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough
  3. Let the dough rise
  4. Bake pizza
