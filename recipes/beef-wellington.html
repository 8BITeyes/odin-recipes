Beef Wellington Recipe

Special Cut of Decadent Meat

Sear the tenderloin, then prepare the mushroom duxelles.

We then wrap the tenderloin and bake the wellington.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 pound beef tenderloin
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 Tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Generously salt and pepper your tenderloin.
  2. In a food processor, add the mushrooms.
  3. Roll out some plastic wrap.
  4. Prehead oven to 400 degrees.
  5. Bake until the pastry is golden.
