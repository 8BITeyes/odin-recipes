Vanilla Cake

Delicious Vanilla Flavor

This homemade vanilla cake recipe isn't short on vanilla flavor!

There's a tablespoon of vanilla extract in the cake itself and in the vanilla buttercream frosting.

Ingredients

  • Cake Flour
  • Baking Powder and Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
  • Oil
  • Vanilla extract
  • Butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Eggs
  • Powdered sugar
  • Heavy cream

Steps

  1. Make the cake layers.
  2. Make the vanilla cake frosting.
  3. Assemble the cake.
